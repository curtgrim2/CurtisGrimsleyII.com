Website
Description
The key word in this website's construction:simplicity. Users from all ages should be able to navigate through this website with ease.

Sometimes less is more and I am of the mindset that an over abundence of resources is unnecessary. I took the minamalist approach in constructing this website so that I wouldn't bombard anyone.

I chose the unknown realms of outer space to be this websites theme because this is my own personal space where I can express myself and showcase my abilities.
